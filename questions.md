# LLM Domain Insights - Example Questions

## 1. **Client Performance Analysis**

Which clients have the highest order volumes this quarter, and what are their most frequently purchased product
categories?

## 2. **Product Category Trends**

Which product categories are showing declining sales trends over the past 6 months, and what might be the potential
reasons?

## 3. **Revenue & Payment Patterns**

What are the payment behavior patterns of our top 10 clients, and which ones have concerning overdue invoice trends?

## 4. **Order Fulfillment Insights**

Which orders are stuck in specific statuses for unusually long periods, and what common characteristics do these delayed
orders share?

## 5. **Account Manager Performance**

How do the different account managers compare in terms of client retention, average order values, and invoice collection
rates?
